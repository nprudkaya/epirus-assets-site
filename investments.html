Why now

The window is defined by supply, not sentiment

Athens and the Aegean islands absorbed the majority of hotel development investment over the last decade. Epirus did not. Tourism arrivals in the region grew 20% year-on-year while the supply of quality accommodation grew at a fraction of that rate.

The assets currently available reflect historical pricing, not the pricing that will follow when institutional capital arrives in volume. That arrival is a matter of when — the infrastructure build-out, the port privatisation, and the sustained demand data make the direction clear.

We work with a small number of clients at any given time and do not take commissions from sellers. Our interest is in matching the right investor to the right asset — not in closing transactions quickly.
