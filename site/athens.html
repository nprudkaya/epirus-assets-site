Overview

Athens Urban Lifestyle Hotel

A nine-storey hotel in central Athens — structural works and external facade completed, active construction permit in place. The asset is available as a full acquisition or as a long-term master lease, making it accessible to two distinct investor profiles with different risk-return expectations.

The building's rooftop delivers a direct, unobstructed view of the Acropolis and the Parthenon — a feature with no substitute in the central Athens hospitality market. Combined with a 300 m² wellness and spa area, 64 rooms across a compact and efficient floor plan, and a location ten minutes from Syntagma Square, the hotel is positioned for the upper end of the four-star and boutique five-star segments.

The shell-and-core status is a structural advantage: it eliminates the primary risk category of hotel development — permitting and structural unknowns — while allowing the acquirer full control over the interior design and brand concept. The total project budget including fit-out is €16,000,000, with projected annual NOI of €1,320,000 and an estimated post-stabilisation market value of €22,000,000.

What makes this asset

Four structural advantages

01

The Acropolis view

A signature rooftop bar and panoramic pool with direct, unobstructed sightlines to the Acropolis and the Parthenon. This is the primary driver of ADR at the top end of the Athens market — a view that cannot be engineered elsewhere in the central location.

02

Strategic location

Situated in the epicentre of Athens tourist and business traffic — 10 minutes to Syntagma, Monastiraki, and the Acropolis, 26 minutes to Piraeus Port. The catchment covers both leisure travellers and the city's corporate demand base.

Shell & core advantage

Structural construction is complete and the active permit is in place. The acquirer inherits all the physical work and regulatory clearance without inheriting structural uncertainty — and retains complete control over interior specification, brand concept, and operator selection.

04

Golden Visa eligibility

The acquisition price meets the Greek Golden Visa threshold applicable to central Athens. The investment functions simultaneously as a real estate holding, a hospitality business, and a residency vehicle — three functions within a single asset.

Fit-out and positioning

What the acquirer controls

Brand and concept

Shell-and-core status gives the acquirer a blank interior. This enables the installation of a fully custom concept — independent boutique, soft-brand affiliation, or operator franchise — without the compromises that come with inheriting an existing fitout.

Technical specification

All HVAC, soundproofing, and smart-room technology is specified from scratch. High-spec infrastructure at the fit-out stage is significantly less expensive than retrofitting an operational hotel — and the difference is permanent, affecting guest experience and operating costs for the life of the asset.

Defensive value

Central Athens real estate is a high-liquidity asset class with consistent institutional demand. The property functions as an inflation hedge and a capital store independent of its hospitality income — a dual-purpose holding for investors who want both yield and balance-sheet protection.
