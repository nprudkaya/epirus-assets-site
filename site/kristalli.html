The concept

Smart boutique hotel in the historic centre

A reconstruction project 150 metres from the Ioannina Fortress, at the intersection of the city's major commercial streets. The asset combines a scarce historic centre address with a fully automated operating model — self check-in, zero staff overhead, modern guest experience.

The architectural concept optimises ~340 m² across five levels for 6 to 8 keys: compact studios on the upper floors, spacious lofts on the lower residential levels, and an exploitable panoramic rooftop. Golden Visa eligible. Privately owned, off-market.

The building

~340 m² across five levels

The reconstruction plan allocates each level to a distinct room type. Upper floors are configured as compact smart-hotel studios with efficient layouts. The first and second floors are converted into spacious lofts that command the highest ADR in the building. The semi-basement offers two configurations depending on investor preference.

The exploitable rooftop is the asset's differentiator: a panoramic lounge area at historic centre elevation, overlooking the fortress and the city. No competing supply in this format exists within the immediate area.

Why invest now

Three reasons to enter at the reconstruction stage

01

Below-market entry and immediate capital gain

Reconstruction-stage acquisition secures a price per square metre below the finished-asset market rate. Historic centre development sites in Ioannina are scarce — asset value increases significantly upon completion. The spread between cost and market value is captured at the point of delivery, not after years of operation.

02

Growing tourism, acute supply shortage

Ioannina is developing as a year-round destination — city break, cultural, and eco-tourism. Amid steady growth in arrivals, there is a sharp shortage of tech-enabled smart-luxury boutique hotels in the historic centre. High occupancy and no direct competition in this format.

03

Golden Visa window before thresholds rise

Against tightening EU residency rules and rising investment thresholds in Athens and the islands, this project remains one of the most capital-efficient vehicles for EU residency. Ioannina falls within the standard €250,000 threshold zone. This project exceeds that threshold and qualifies.
