Overview

Heritage Residence

A three-level neoclassical stone mansion in the heart of Ioannina's historic district, 100 metres from the castle and the lake. The building is listed by the Greek Ministry of Culture, fully restored between 2008 and 2013, and offered off-market with a complete documentation package ready for transaction.

The asset carries the rarity premium of a heritage title in the city's most prestigious location — a category where new supply is structurally impossible. The internal reinforced concrete frame provides structural longevity without compromising the authentic stone facade that defines the property's identity.

The investment case offers two distinct scenarios. A conservative long-term rental model provides a stable, predictable 5% yield with minimal management burden and clear Golden Visa eligibility. A high-yield management model — operating the upper floors as serviced apartments — targets 13% ROI with 19.7% return on leveraged equity.
