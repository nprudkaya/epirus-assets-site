The concept

Automated boutique hotel in the historic core

A tightly scoped optimisation project 200 metres from the Byzantine Castle, at the intersection of Ioannina's primary commercial streets. The asset combines a protected historic centre address with a fully automated operating model — IoT keyless access, zero permanent staff, cloud-managed housekeeping.

The renovation plan unlocks 283 m² across four above-ground levels for exactly 6 keys: 2 spacious lower apartments and 4 compact upper studios, plus a 51.94 m² open rooftop terrace. Two income strategies modelled: boutique hotel at 9.13% ROI or corporate long-term rental at 5.61% ROI. Golden Visa eligible. Off-market.

The building

283 m² across four levels

The renovation scope is precisely calibrated to the existing inventory. The upper two residential floors house 4 compact studios requiring only cosmetic refresh — updated finishes and FF&E. The ground floor and mezzanine undergo full conversion into two premium apartments, including a highly desirable private street-level entrance on the ground floor.

The 51.94 m² open rooftop terrace — accessible by both lift and central staircase — serves as a secluded courtyard lounge deck with authentic views over Ioannina's historic tiled roofs. No structural extension is required.

Why invest now

Three reasons to enter at the reconstruction stage

01

Below-market entry at €2,290/m²

Acquisition at €650,000 represents an entry cost of just €2,290 per gross m² in a protected historic centre location where new supply is structurally constrained. The renovation scope is tightly scoped to net interior surfaces — averaging €1,300/m² on full conversions and €400/m² on cosmetic refresh — making the all-in CAPEX of €822,365 highly optimised relative to comparable finished assets.

02

Growing tourism, acute supply shortage

Ioannina is developing as a year-round destination — city break, cultural, and eco-tourism. Amid steady growth in arrivals, there is a sharp shortage of tech-enabled smart-luxury boutique hotels in the historic centre. High occupancy and no direct competition in this format.
