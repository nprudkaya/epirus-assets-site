Exclusive off-market opportunity.

**Location:** Ioannina Downtown, Greece.
**Asset:** 3-level traditional stone building (listed heritage site) of 343 sq.m. on a 250.86 sq.m. plot.
**Status:** Operational / Fully Equipped. Restored between 2008–2013. Full documentation package ready for transaction.

## 1. Unique selling proposition

- **Historic Authenticity:** A rare example of a neoclassical stone mansion protected by the Greek Ministry of Culture. A "trophy asset" in the city's most prestigious district.
- **Prime Location:** 100 meters from the Ioannina Castle and Lake Pamvotis. Maximum tourist foot traffic guaranteed.
- **Modern Engineering:** Internal reinforced concrete frame combined with the preserved authentic stone facade for structural safety and longevity.
- **Flexible Layout:** Modernized to accommodate spacious apartments, highly sought after by premium travelers and digital nomads.
