Golden Visa

Greek residency through property ownership

01
Purchase from €250,000
Paragon I qualifies under Zone B — the lowest qualifying price point for central Athens. Confirm zone classification at the time of purchase.
02
Submit the GV application
Filed with the Greek Migration Ministry after completion. Processing typically 3–6 months. A local lawyer is required — Epirus Assets can refer one.
03
Receive 5-year residency permit
Renewable indefinitely while the property is held. Covers the buyer, spouse, and dependent children under 21.
04
Schengen travel access
Visa-free travel across 26 Schengen countries. No minimum stay in Greece required to maintain the permit.

Zone note: The €250,000 threshold applies to Zone B. Zone A — most Aegean islands, southern Athens suburbs, Thessaloniki — now requires €800,000. Gazi currently falls in Zone B, but zone boundaries are reviewed periodically. Verify current status before proceeding.
