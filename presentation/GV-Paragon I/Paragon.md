## Paragon I — Technopolis Residences, Gazi Athens

### Property Overview

Paragon I is a commercial-to-residential conversion delivering 20 one-bedroom apartments in Gazi — Athens' most active arts, technology, and nightlife district. Located on Pireos Boulevard at the boundary of Gazi and Thiseio, the building sits 400 metres from Kerameikos Metro and within 12 minutes of the Acropolis. The project spans 8 storeys with units from 39 m² studios to a 129 m² penthouse with a private 88 m² terrace and direct Acropolis views. Delivered turn-key to energy class A standard.
