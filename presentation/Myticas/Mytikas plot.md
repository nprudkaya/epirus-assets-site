# Investment teaser: Mytikas Seafront Estate

Exclusive development project on the Ionian seafront.

**Location:** Mytikas, Preveza, Greece.
**Asset:** Land plot of 11,326 m² on a unique natural 8-meter rock elevation.
**Status:** Independent development project. Privately owned land, full documentation package ready for transaction.

## 1. Unique selling proposition

- **"Amphitheater Effect":** Situated on a natural plateau 8 meters above sea level, guaranteeing unobstructed sunset and panoramic sea views for 100% of the guestrooms.
- **Privacy & Security:** The elevation provides a natural barrier, ensuring absolute privacy and safety from coastal erosion or climate-related sea-level changes.
- **Scarce Asset:** The Mytikas coastline is almost fully developed; this plot represents one of the last remaining opportunities for large-scale high-end development.
- **Strategic Access:** 15 minutes from Aktion International Airport (PVK), 8 km from Preveza town and its premium marina.

## 2. Project parameters

- **Land Acquisition Cost:** €2,900,000
- **Commercial Building Capacity:** 2,265 m² (above-ground GFA) + extensive basement levels
- **Target Segment:** Smart-Luxury / Eco-Design Boutique Hotel

## 3. Development scenarios

### Scenario A: "Premium Combo"

*Focus on maximum ADR (Average Daily Rate) and brand capitalization.*

- **Inventory:** 42 Keys (24 Standards at 35 m² + 18 Suites at 45–60 m² with private infinity pools).
- **Total CAPEX:** ~€9.75M.
- **Projected Annual EBITDA:** ~€1.57M.
- **Value Add:** Creation of a "Trophy Asset." The integration of 18 private pools on a cliffside makes the property unique in the region, significantly driving exit valuation.

### Scenario B: "Maximum Capacity"

*Focus on rapid ROI and maximized operational cash flow.*

- **Inventory:** 52 Keys (50 Standards at 30 m² + 2 Flagship Grand Suites at 50 m²).
- **Total CAPEX:** ~€10.0M.
- **Projected Annual EBITDA:** ~€1.75M.
- **Value Add:** Highest yield on invested capital (17%+) through optimized OPEX and a larger volume of inventory units.

## 5. Why invest now?

The Preveza region is currently in a high-growth phase as an emerging luxury destination. The completion of new highway links and the expansion of PVK Airport ensure a steady increase in high-net-worth European travelers. Entering the project at this stage allows the investor to secure prime beachfront land and achieve yields significantly higher than the EU market average.
