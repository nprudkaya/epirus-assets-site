# Kastro Gate Automated Suites — Ioannina Historic Core

## Property Overview

A freestanding commercial/residential building in the historic core of Ioannina, 200 metres from the Byzantine Castle, being converted into a 6-key fully automated boutique hotel or corporate housing asset. Built in 1989 under strict post-seismic Greek construction standards — no structural remediation required. The renovation scope is tightly optimised: deep conversion only on the ground floor, cosmetic refresh on the existing upper studios, and a 51.94 m² rooftop terrace fit-out as a private lounge deck.

## Unique Selling Points

- **Structurally de-risked entry at €2,290/m²:** 1989 reinforced concrete frame meets post-1985 seismic code — no foundation or column remediation — while the tightly scoped CAPEX (€1,300/m² on reconstruction, €400/m² on cosmetic refresh) keeps total investment well below replacement cost in a location with zero new supply.
- **Full automation embedded from day one:** IoT keyless access, cloud-managed housekeeping dispatch, and digital guest operations eliminate permanent on-site staff; OPEX is capped at 30% of revenue under the hotel model, making the 9.13% yield structurally defensible even at conservative 60% occupancy.
