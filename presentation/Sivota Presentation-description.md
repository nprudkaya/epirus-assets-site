# Sivota Waterfront Apart-Hotel — Ionian Coast, Emerald of Epirus

## 📝 Property Overview

A three-level waterfront building 100 metres from the Sivota Marina, being repositioned as a premium boutique apart-hotel targeting the Ionian yachting community and high-end European summer tourism. The 11-unit inventory is structured across three tiers — 2 optimised studios (ground floor, budget/high-occupancy), 8 luxury guest rooms (primary revenue drivers), and 1 penthouse premium apartment — offering a diversified room mix that maximises both occupancy rate and ADR. Under professional hospitality management (Strategy B), the asset targets 75% occupancy at an ADR of €175, delivering a 10% net yield on total invested capital.
