## Cosmo I — Between the City and the Sea, Neos Kosmos Athens

### Property Overview

Cosmo I is a commercial-to-residential conversion delivering 10 energy-efficient apartments in Neos Kosmos — an established residential district of Athens positioned between the historic city centre and the Athenian Riviera coastline. Located 270 metres from Neos Kosmos Metro Station (Line 2), the building offers direct access to both Syntagma and the southern coastal areas without reliance on a car. The project spans five storeys with a mix of one- and two-bedroom layouts, from 31 m² one-beds to a 37 m² top-floor unit with a 30.90 m² private rooftop terrace. Delivered turn-key to energy class A standard, with optional furnished packages enabling immediate rental activation.
