# Investment teaser: Project Gastro-Eco Ecosystem

Confidential off-market opportunity: Boutique Gastro Hotel.

**Location:** Ioannina, Greece.
**Asset:** Mixed-use hospitality building of approximately 350 sq.m.
**Status:** Ready-to-operate concept. Privately owned, full documentation package available for transaction.

## 1. Unique selling proposition

- **Dual-Income Model:** A self-sustaining hospitality ecosystem combining 5 premium boutique suites with a high-margin ground-floor gastro-hub.
- **Year-Round Stability:** Unlike seasonal coastal resorts, this asset leverages Ioannina's status as a year-round administrative, educational, and medical center.
- **Strategic Synergy:** The in-house gastro-hub serves both hotel guests and the high-density local population (University students and tech professionals), ensuring diversified revenue streams.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Designed for modern management with minimal staff overhead, utilizing automated guest access systems.

## 4. Key investor advantages

1. **Silicon Valley of Greece:** Ioannina is a booming international IT cluster (TeamViewer, Deloitte, PwC), driving a severe deficit in premium accommodations.
2. **University Proximity:** Located near major educational institutions, ensuring a stable, high-quality tenant base for the F&B operations.
3. **Asset Appreciation:** Expected growth potential of 15–20% over a 3-year horizon due to regional infrastructure expansion.
4. **Golden Visa Eligible:** The asset price point and structure are fully compliant with Greek Golden Visa residency regulations.
