# Kastro Quarter Boutique Hotel — Ioannina City Centre

## Property Overview

A fully repositionable urban hospitality asset positioned at the premier commercial intersection of Ioannina, exactly 200 metres from the Byzantine Castle (Kastro) — the city's defining landmark and primary cultural draw. The building spans four above-ground levels plus a rooftop, delivering 16 keys / 35 beds across professionally designed rooms, a reception lobby with in-house bar, an automated laundry hub, and a 90 m² sheltered rooftop terrace with authentic views over the historic tiled-roof skyline. The asset has been assessed under two distinct operating strategies — boutique hotel and long-term corporate rental — both modelled against verified architectural plans, offering an investor a clear, data-supported entry thesis.
