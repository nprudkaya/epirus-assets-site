# Investment teaser: Boutique Hotel in Mytikas

Hospitality project in Preveza, Greece.

## Project overview

- **Location:** Mytikas (Preveza), Ionian Coast. 15 min from Aktion International Airport (PVK).
- **Asset:** Seafront Luxury Boutique Hotel (4-star luxury category).
- **Status:** Under construction (approx. 70% complete). Concrete structures and core systems installed.
- **Current Plot Area:** 1,125 sq.m.
- **Current Development (Phase 1):** 20 luxury suites.

## Unique selling proposition

- **Natural Cape Elevation:** Offers unobstructed 180° sunset views and unique privacy for guests.
- **Eco-Luxury Differentiation:** Minimalist architectural design featuring luxury suites with private pools or waterfall features.
- **Scalability:** Potential to increase capacity to 32 keys, which grows revenue by 57% while operating expenses (OPEX) increase by only 20%.

## Why invest now?

- **Low Construction Risk:** The project is already 70% structurally complete. Major risks associated with permits and core construction are behind, ensuring a faster path to operation.
- **Government Backed Safety:** Includes an approved state subsidy (cash-back) of €500,000, significantly reducing the effective capital requirement.
- **Immediate Value Appreciation:** Investors capture the "Developer's Margin" (~€1.2M) immediately upon project completion, as the market value of the finished asset exceeds the total cost of construction.
- **Prime Seafront Scarcity:** Direct seafront land with a private cape on the Ionian coast is a finite resource, ensuring long-term asset value protection and high exit demand.
- **Strategic Timing:** Preveza is emerging as a high-end alternative to overcrowded Greek islands, benefiting from the new highway infrastructure and increased international flight connectivity.
- **Projected exit value after 5 years of operation: €15,000,000** (based on Scenario C scaling).
