# Papazoglou Mansion — Trophy Value-Add, Historic Trade Center, Ioannina

## 📝 Property Overview

A freehold, detached listed mansion on Ioannina's most prestigious commercial artery, offering a sophisticated value-add development play: full structural restoration of 457 m² of built area (ground floor + upper floor + attic) plus a 200 m² private courtyard garden. The conversion strategy targets a flagship gastronomy destination on the ground floor and three ultra-luxury lofts (85–90 m² each, 4-metre ceilings) on the upper floors — purpose-built for the region's expanding IT executive market. The asset's listed status, exceptional ceiling height, and private garden constitute a combination that cannot be replicated within the dense historic centre.
