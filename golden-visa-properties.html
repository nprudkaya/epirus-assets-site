Why Epirus

The most cost-effective Golden Visa entry point in Greece

Epirus falls within the standard Golden Visa threshold zone — €250,000 — unlike Athens, Thessaloniki, Mykonos, and Santorini, which are subject to the €800,000 requirement introduced in 2023. That €550,000 difference is not a discount: it is the same residency right, the same five-year permit, the same path to citizenship — at a fraction of the capital commitment.

For investors whose primary goal is residency rather than yield, Epirus is the most capital-efficient route available in Greece. For investors who want both residency and income, the region's growing hospitality market provides that combination at an entry price that remains accessible.
